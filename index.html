About

A passion for teaching mathematics

I'm Tarek Amer — a math educator who believes every student can love math when it's taught with patience, precision, and the right method. I teach the Ministerial, American, and British curricula in English, with modern online tools and proven strategies.

From foundational reviews for grades 8–12 to full Digital SAT preparation and university-level Calculus, Statistics, and Microeconomics — every session is designed around clarity and confidence.
